Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 09, 2026

1. Who we are

EcoDrop ("we", "us", "our") operates the website ecodrop.myshopify.com. We are the data controller for your personal data.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to process your order:

• Name and email address (communication)
• Shipping address (delivery)
• Payment reference (processed by Stripe, we never see your card number)
• Order history (customer service)
• IP address (fraud prevention, not stored beyond session)

3. Legal basis

We process your data under:
• Art. 6(1)(b) RGPD — contract performance (order processing)
• Art. 6(1)(c) RGPD — legal obligation (tax records, 10 years)
• Art. 6(1)(a) RGPD — consent (marketing emails, if you opt in)
• Art. 6(1)(f) RGPD — legitimate interest (fraud prevention)

4. Third parties

• Stripe (payment processing, EU-hosted, PCI-DSS Level 1)
• Shopify (store hosting, may process in US with SCCs)
• Shipping carrier (name + address for delivery)

5. Data retention

• Order data: 10 years (legal obligation, Code de commerce)
• Marketing data: until you unsubscribe
• Account data: until you request deletion

6. Your rights

You have the right to: access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, and restriction. Contact us at support@ecodrop.myshopify.com. You may also complain to your local data protection authority (CNIL in France: cnil.fr).

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies (shopping cart, session). No analytics or marketing cookies without your consent. You can reject non-essential cookies via our cookie banner.

8. International transfers

If your data is processed outside the EU (e.g., Shopify in US/Canada), we use Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure RGPD-level protection.
